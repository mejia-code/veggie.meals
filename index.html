Seaweed

Seaweed is a plant that grows in the sea. It is a very common food in Japan, Korea, and Taiwan. It is also a very common food in the Mediterranean region.

Seaweed is a source of many nutrients, such as protein, calcium, and vitamin C. It is also a source of essential fatty acids, including omega-3 which are essential for the body to maintain a healthy weight.
